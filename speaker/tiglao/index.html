Dr. Noriel Christopher C. Tiglao

Associate Professor, National College of Public Administration & Governance, University of the Philippines Diliman

Speaker Profile

"Dr. Noriel Christopher C. Tiglao, a civil engineer by profession, is an Associate Professor at the National College of Public Administration & Governance (NCPAG). He is also a senior fellow of the Pimentel Institute for Leadership & Governance and has served as the Director of the Center for Policy and Executive Development (CPED), the research arm of NCPAG concerned with policy and executive studies.
