Dr. Rogel Mari Sese

Program Leader at National SPACE Development Program

Speaker Profile

One of the few astrophysicists in the Philippines, Dr. Sese is currently the country's representative in the International Astronomical Union, Galileo Teacher Training Program and the Universe Awareness Program.

Also, Dr. Sese is the Chairperson of the Southeast Asian Young Astronomers Collaboration and President of Regulus Spacetech Inc.

https://ph.linkedin.com/in/rogel-mari-sese-5309bbb0


Creating a National Space Agency: Current Development and Future Challenges

April 20, 2016 | 10:30-11:00am

With the upcoming launch of the DIWATA-1 microsatellite, the Philippines has made significant steps in developing space science and technology applications (SSTA). In this talk, I will discuss the recent developments in SSTA such as the crafting of the National Space Development and Utilization Policy and the National SPACE Development Program (NSDP). I will also discuss the various roadmaps for the next ten (10) years currently being drafted under the NSDP that will lead to establishing the foundations of a future National Space Agency. Finally, I will highlight issues regarding the future of SSTA in the Philippines and how it is being addressed in order to ensure the long-term sustainability of the Philippine space program.
